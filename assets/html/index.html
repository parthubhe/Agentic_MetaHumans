Bridging AI and Virtual Reality

Welcome to Vocal Persona by Code Slayers, where we redefine the interaction between humans and artificial intelligence. We create sophisticated AI agentic chatbots and bring them to life using stunningly realistic MetaHumans within the powerful Unreal Engine environment.

Our innovation doesn't stop at visuals. Leveraging Nvidia's cutting-edge Audio2Face technology, we generate precise, real-time lip-sync animations directly from the AI's generated speech, ensuring natural and believable conversations.

The true magic lies in our custom emotion classification engine. It intelligently analyzes the nuances in both the user's input and the AI's response to dynamically tailor the MetaHuman's facial expressions and animations. This creates an unprecedented level of empathetic and engaging virtual interaction, moving beyond text to truly virtualize the agentic AI experience.
